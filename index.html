Goal Force: Teaching Video Models To Accomplish Physics-Conditioned Goals

CVPR, 2026

Goal Force specifies visual goals for video world models via explicit force vectors; trained only on synthetic causal primitives, it generalizes zero-shot to real-world scenes as an implicit neural physics simulator.
