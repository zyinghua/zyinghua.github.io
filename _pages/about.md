Welcome to my site! I am currently a CS master's student at <a href="https://www.brown.edu/">Brown University</a>.

I am interested in: 1. **Embodied Intelligence**, particularly large foundation models for generalist robot learning, such as [VLA](https://www.pi.website/blog/pi0) and [WAM](https://dreamzero0.github.io/). 2. **Visual Intelligence**, especially video generation as a route to learning the physics, dynamics, and compositional structure of the visual world.

Prior to Brown, I completed my B.S. in Computer Science at <a href="https://www.monash.edu/">Monash University</a> 
and received my (first class) Honours degree in Computer Science from <a href="https://www.sydney.edu.au/">The University of Sydney</a>.

Outside of work, I enjoy playing video games 🎮, watching movies 🎥, and playing sports like table tennis 🏓, badminton 🏸, and so on. I'm also deeply interested in AI entrepreneurship!
